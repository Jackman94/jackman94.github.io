About

We have always wanted to create a quality product which would be competitive in the global market. It took us a long time to select the right people from various bands. As a result, we became a great team in August 2017.

We are inspired by such bands as Periphery, Crystal lake, Bring me the horizon and Polaris. We are big fans of Architects. In particular, we admire their strong unified team, their friendship and their desire to speak through their music, about global matters which concern everyone.
